SPONSORS

We welcome sponsorship and exhibition support for the 2027 IACMAG Symposium. The symposium provides an excellent opportunity to showcase your organisation, products, and services to an international audience of researchers, engineers, and industry professionals.

Call for Sponsors

There are currently two types of sponsorship opportunities available for the 2027 IACMAG Symposium. All sponsors will be provided with an exhibition table during the conference.

Platinum Sponsorship, USD 10,000

  • 2 tables in exhibition area
  • 4 complimentary conference registrations
  • Complimentary lunch, coffee breaks, and banquet dinner
  • Acknowledgement during the opening and closing ceremony
  • Logo on the symposium website with hyperlink
  • Logo in social media promotion for the symposium
  • Logo in booklet, flyer, and proceedings
  • Logo on the main symposium poster

Gold Sponsorship, USD 8,000

  • 1 table in exhibition area
  • 2 complimentary conference registrations
  • Complimentary lunch, coffee breaks, and banquet dinner
  • Acknowledgement during the opening and closing ceremony
  • Logo on the symposium website with hyperlink
  • Logo in social media promotion for the symposium
  • Logo in booklet, flyer, and proceedings
  • Logo on the main symposium poster

Call for Exhibits

There are currently two exhibit opportunities available for organisations wishing to present products, services, brochures, and business materials during the symposium.

Table Exhibit, USD 3,000

  • 1 table in exhibition area
  • 3 complimentary conference registrations
  • Complimentary lunch, coffee breaks, and banquet dinner (for three people)
  • Acknowledgement during the opening and closing ceremony

1-Day Table Exhibitor, USD 1,500

  • 1 table in exhibition area
  • 1-day complimentary conference registration
  • Complimentary lunch, coffee breaks, and banquet dinner (for three people)
  • Acknowledgement during the opening and closing ceremony

The exhibit provides a platform for companies and institutions to present their products and services, and also offers valuable networking opportunities with researchers and professionals in computational mechanics, geomechanics, multiphysics coupling, and related engineering applications.
